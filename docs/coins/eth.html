Analysis

Strong Alpha Despite Negative Return

While the strategy returned -0.71%, the market (Buy & Hold) lost -19.66%. This represents significant alpha of +18.95%.

Why ETH Underperforms BTC

  • Less training data - OI data only available since Dec 2021 (vs Sep 2020 for BTC)
  • Higher volatility - ETH has larger price swings, harder to predict
  • More trades - 140 trades vs 100 for BTC, more exposure to fees/slippage
  • Bucket sizing - $25 buckets may be suboptimal for ETH price range

Capital Preservation

The strategy protected capital effectively:

  • Lost only 0.71% vs 19.66% market decline
  • Max drawdown of 2.37% is manageable
  • Profit factor of 1.03 means near breakeven trading
