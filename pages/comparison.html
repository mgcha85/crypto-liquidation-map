Strategy Characteristics

🧠 PPO Strengths

  • Directly optimizes for PnL, not accuracy
  • Learns complex temporal patterns via CNN
  • Adapts position sizing through portfolio state
  • Higher trade frequency captures more opportunities
  • Excellent bear market performance (2022: +327% alpha)

🌳 XGBoost Strengths

  • Interpretable feature importance
  • Fast training and inference
  • Robust to overfitting with regularization
  • Lower trade frequency = lower costs
  • Established in production environments

Recommendation

🏆 Use PPO for Live Trading

Higher alpha (+129.6% avg), consistent Sharpe (7.32 avg), and robust across market regimes. Recommended for automated trading.

📊 Use XGBoost for Research

Interpretable features help understand market dynamics. Good baseline for feature engineering and hypothesis testing.
